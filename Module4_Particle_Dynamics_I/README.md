# Module 4 – Particle Dynamics I: Classical Motion and Conservation Laws

This module introduces computational modeling of classical particle dynamics based on Newtonian mechanics.

Students simulate motion of particles and analyze physical laws governing motion.

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module students will be able to:

• implement Newton’s equations of motion computationally  
• simulate linear and nonlinear motion of particles  
• analyze work-energy relationships  
• study collision dynamics  
• verify conservation laws using simulations  

---

## Topics Covered

Newton’s equations of motion  

Work–energy theorem  

Conservative forces  

Linear motion  

Non-linear motion  

Uniform motion  

Non-uniform motion  

Collision of particles  

Elastic collision  

Inelastic collision  

Conservation laws
