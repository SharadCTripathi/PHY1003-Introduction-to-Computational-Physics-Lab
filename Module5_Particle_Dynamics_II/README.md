# Module 5 – Particle Dynamics II: Oscillatory Systems

This module focuses on computational modeling of oscillatory motion in physical systems.

Students will study harmonic and anharmonic oscillations and analyze the influence of external forces and damping.

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module students will be able to:

• simulate simple harmonic motion  
• analyze energy and phase of oscillatory systems  
• model coupled oscillators  
• study anharmonic oscillations  
• simulate motion in dissipative environments  

---

## Topics Covered

Simple harmonic motion  

Simple harmonic oscillator  

Energy of simple harmonic oscillator  

Phase of simple harmonic oscillator  

Two-body harmonic oscillator  

Anharmonic oscillations  

Motion in a frictional environment  

Motion in a drag environment
